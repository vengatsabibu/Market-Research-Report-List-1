<p><p>The water pollution control equipment market is categorized into two main types based on contaminants: particulate and chemical. Particulate contaminant control involves equipment designed to remove solid particles, such as sediments and debris, from water sources, often utilizing filters and sedimentation tanks. Chemical contaminant control focuses on addressing dissolved pollutants, including heavy metals and toxins, through advanced methods like chemical treatments, ion exchange, and reverse osmosis. Both types are essential for maintaining water quality and ensuring environmental protection.</p></p>
