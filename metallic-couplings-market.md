<p><p>The metallic couplings market includes various types such as chain and gear couplings, which serve as essential components in mechanical systems. Chain couplings use linked components to transmit torque and accommodate misalignment, making them suitable for power transmission in heavy machinery. Gear couplings, on the other hand, utilize interlocking gears to provide a flexible connection, allowing for angular misalignment while ensuring smooth power transfer. Both types enhance efficiency and reliability in diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
