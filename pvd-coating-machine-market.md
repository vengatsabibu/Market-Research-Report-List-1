<p><p>The PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) coating machine market is segmented into types based on the equipment used: evaporation equipment, sputtering equipment, and others. </p><p>Evaporation equipment utilizes thermal techniques to vaporize materials, allowing them to condense onto substrates for coating. Sputtering equipment, on the other hand, ejects material from a target surface through ion bombardment, enabling precise layer deposition. Other types of equipment may include specialized techniques like laser ablation or ion plating, catering to diverse industrial applications and coating requirements.</p></p>
