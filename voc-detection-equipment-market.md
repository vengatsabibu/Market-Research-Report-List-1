<p><strong>The VOC Detection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC detection equipment market primarily includes two types: Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID sensors utilize ultraviolet light to ionize VOCs, enabling real-time detection with high sensitivity, suitable for hazardous environments. In contrast, MOS sensors detect VOCs through changes in resistance when exposed to specific gases, offering a cost-effective and compact solution for indoor air quality monitoring. Both technologies serve vital roles in environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and health applications.</p></p>
