<p><p>Inline samplers are vital devices used in various industries for collecting representative samples of bulk materials. The market is primarily divided into two types: manual and pneumatic. Manual type inline samplers require operator intervention for sampling, making them ideal for smaller operations or where automation is not feasible. In contrast, pneumatic type samplers use compressed air for automated sampling, enhancing efficiency and consistency in larger production environments. Both types serve essential roles in quality control and process monitoring across industries.</p></p>
