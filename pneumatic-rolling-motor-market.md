<p><strong>The Pneumatic Rolling Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Torque Controlled</li><li>Stall Torque</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic rolling motor market is primarily categorized into two types: torque-controlled and stall torque motors. Torque-controlled motors are designed to maintain a constant torque output, allowing for precise control during operations, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent performance. Stall torque motors, on the other hand, are characterized by their ability to deliver maximum torque at standstill, providing high starting power. Both types play crucial roles in various industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and productivity in processes involving rolling and conveyor systems.</p></p>
