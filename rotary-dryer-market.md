<p><ul><li>Direct-heated Rotary Dryer</li><li>Indirect Rotary Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary dryer market primarily consists of two types: direct-heated and indirect rotary dryers. Direct-heated rotary dryers utilize a direct flame or hot gas to heat materials, providing efficient moisture removal and high throughput. In contrast, indirect rotary dryers heat materials through heated drum walls, preventing direct contact with combustion gases, which is ideal for processing heat-sensitive materials. Both types cater to various industries, including agriculture, mining, and food processing, tailored to specific drying needs and material characteristics.</p></p>
