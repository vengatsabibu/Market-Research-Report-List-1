<p><p>The plastic pelletizers market is categorized into two primary types: wet cut and dry cut. Wet cut pelletizers use water during the cutting process, which helps in cooling and transporting plastic pellets, making them suitable for high-output applications. In contrast, dry cut pelletizers operate without water, focusing on a more energy-efficient process with less contamination risk. Each type caters to specific production needs in plastic recycling and manufacturing, influencing efficiency, quality, and operational costs.</p></p>
