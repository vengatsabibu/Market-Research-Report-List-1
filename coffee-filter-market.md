<p><ul><li>Permanent Filters</li><li>Paper Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coffee filter market is primarily divided into two types: permanent filters and paper filters. Permanent filters are typically made of metal or plastic and are reusable, offering durability and cost-effectiveness over time. They retain essential oils in coffee, enhancing flavor. On the other hand, paper filters are disposable and convenient, ensuring a clean brew by trapping fine coffee particles and oils. While paper filters require regular replacement, they appeal to users seeking ease and a quick clean-up after brewing.</p></p>
