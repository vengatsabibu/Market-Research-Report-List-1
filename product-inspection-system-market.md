Metal detectors identify metal foreign bodies in food or materials, enhancing product reliability. X-ray machines provide comprehensive scans to detect a variety of contaminants, including non-metallic objects. Together, these technologies play a crucial role in maintaining product integrity and compliance in various industries, particularly food and packaging.</p></p>
